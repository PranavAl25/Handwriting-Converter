About the Web App

Understanding Dysgraphia

Dysgraphia is a learning disability that affects a person's ability to write coherently and legibly. It impacts nearly 20%, or 1 in 5, of individuals around the world. Individuals with dysgraphia can struggle with self-esteem and experience social anxiety.

The Role of Therapy

Individuals with dysgraphia often seek therapy to improve their writing skills and cope with the challenges they face. Therapy may involve various approaches such as occupational therapy, handwriting exercises, and cognitive-behavioral techniques. However these treatments are very expensive, hence needing a solution that can let individuals live with dysgraphia lifelong.

The Dysgraphia Web App

The Dysgraphia Web App provides an innovative solution to individuals with dysgraphia. By converting typed text into personalized handwriting, the app helps users create legible notes and written content. This technology aims to empower individuals with dysgraphia and enhance their communication.

How It Works

Uploading Images

Users can upload images of their handwriting for both uppercase and lowercase letters. Special character images, such as punctuation marks, are also uploaded. This collection of images forms the basis for creating personalized handwriting.

Processing the Content

The uploaded images are processed to create a library of characters. Each character image is associated with its corresponding letter or special character. This library is used to generate the personalized handwriting.

Generating Lined Paper

The app takes the user's uploaded text content and places each character's handwritten image onto a lined paper background. The result is a downloadable image that resembles handwritten notes on lined paper.

Why It Matters

Self-Esteem and Confidence

Individuals with dysgraphia often struggle with their self-esteem due to difficulties in handwriting. The ability to create legible notes using personalized handwriting can boost their confidence and sense of self-worth.

Academic Performance

Handwriting difficulties can impact academic performance and test-taking. By using the Dysgraphia Web App, individuals can better express their knowledge and ideas, leading to improved academic outcomes.

Enhancing Communication

Effective communication is essential in various aspects of life. The app enables individuals with dysgraphia to communicate more clearly and confidently, fostering better interactions with peers, educators, and employers.
